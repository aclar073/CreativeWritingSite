Healing

Written by Amanda Clark

Tear down that wall,

Piece by piece

Flood them with your words

Gift them your grievances until nothing remains

You are not at fault

Let their guilt crash against them

Carrying them from their lives of comfort

Be silent no more

For they cannot tame you

You are free

And the world awaits you

About the Story

This was my first attempt at writing poetry. I wanted to create a poem in honor of victims of trauma. It is a common occurance that victims can be blamed for what happened to them or told not to speak about their experiences. I wanted to challenge that idea though poetry.
